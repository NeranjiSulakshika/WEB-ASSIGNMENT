SEA SIDE
SOUTH PARK HOTEL







   "Sri Lanka’s iconic landmark, The Sea Side South Park Hotel, is situated in the heart of Colombo, along the seafront.
One of the oldest hotels east of the Suez, The Sea Side South Park Hotel embraces its rich history and legendary traditions, utilizing them to create engaging, immersive experiences that resonate with old and new generations of travelers alike.
No visit to Sri Lanka is complete without staying at this majestic hotel, built in 1868 and recently restored back to its former glory."

" ROOMS "

Your comfort is our priority. So, each of the rooms, across our hotels in Sri Lanka is equipped with the best of amenities in a blissful ambience. While the beauty of our scenic locations seeps into every corner and makes the atmosphere come alive, the thoughtful aesthetics make sure you truly enjoy staying with us.


" UNDERWATER DINNER "

Dine five meters below the surface of the ocean, in the world’s first undersea restaurant, with panoramic coral garden views and fusion menus matched with fine wines. It can be reserved for private dining, weddings and special occasions.

" WEDDINGS "

A wedding day at the Grand Ballroom or Jubilee Ballroom of the Sea Side South Park Hotel is a truly memorable and joyous occasion. Elegant surroundings, subtle embellishments and discreet attention will always remind you of the best day of your life.

" BAR "

Rightly acclaimed for a sophisticated and creative cocktail list that is imbued with the spirit of famous travellers past who have graced our shores. A full range of spirituous liquors and fermented libations are offered.
