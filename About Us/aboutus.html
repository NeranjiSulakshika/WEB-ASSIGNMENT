ABOUT US



The Sea Side South Park Hotel opened its doors with a splendid tale of continual improvement of product and the highest standard of quality in hospitality like the exceptional services offered by its well-trained staff and its popularity as a venue of weddings.


The architecture and aesthetic complement the beauty of natural surroundings. Sea Side South Park Hotel is a blissful paradise with genuine connection to the villages and local culture. This connection is sensitively nurtured and shared with our guests seeking experiences beyond the ordinary.


Why book with Sea Side South Park Hotel?

The Sea Side South Park Hotel is the finest star class beach resort in Colombo, Sri Lanka along with the sea front with the best dinning accomodation and entertainment facilities. At Sea Side South Park Hotel we promise you are in good hands so you can discover extra-ordinary places and unwind in our spaces.


With us you can engaged in so many activities like surfing , fishing and beach walks. Also you can watch whales and dolphins jump , or you can sit on the terrace of your villa overlooking the sea and see the sun kissing the waves and passing by while enjoying a snack and a drink.

We will ensure your stay is perfect and your experiences are completed.
